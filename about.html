What this is

The grid, online

ROK Racers JA connects promoters, drivers, and fans in one place. Track nights, gas station link-ups, mountain runs — every meet can live on the same grid instead of getting lost in stories and chats.

Use the Garage to show off your build, attach it to an event when you RSVP, and keep a living history of where your car has been.

  • Find events fast
  • RSVP and show your build
  • See island sightings and threads
  • Support local teams and promoters
How to use it

Core flows

1) Browse events → open event → RSVP (and attach your build).

2) Create a build → make it public → climb into the Top Builds.

3) Drop sightings → start a thread → keep the island feed alive.
