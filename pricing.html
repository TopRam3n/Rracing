Common Questions
How do I pay?
Right now upgrades are handled manually — tap the upgrade button and you'll be directed to contact us via Instagram or email. We're integrating Stripe payments soon.
What's a Build QR Card?
A print-ready card with a unique QR code for your build. Stick it on your dash, under the hood, or hand it out at meets. Anyone who scans it lands on your full build page with specs and 3D model.
Can I cancel?
Yes, contact us anytime. Your Pro features stay active until the end of your billing period.
I'm a promoter — which plan?
Elite is built for you. It includes event creation, go-live access, and team management. Promoter accounts can also be granted manually if you've already requested a team page.
