What ROK Racers JA Is

A digital paddock for Jamaican motorsport — events, builds, sightings and the crews that make it all run.

The grid, online

ROK Racers JA connects promoters, drivers and fans in one place. Track nights, gas station link-ups, mountain runs — every meet can live on the same grid instead of getting lost in stories and chats.

Use the Garage to show off your builds, attach them to events, and keep a living history of where your car has been. The goal is simple: make it easier to see what’s on, who’s outside, and what’s being built across the island.

ROC · RRXNG · Community

Built in Jamaica, for Jamaica. ROK Racers JA is the spine for ROC Mobile Mechanics, RRXNG Club events, and future partner crews. Over time, the platform grows into:

  • Ticketing & RSVP tools for promoters
  • Parts & vendor discovery for builds
  • Mechanic and inspection bookings
  • Stories and features on local drivers

For now, we’re focused on getting the core right: clean events, real builds, and a feed that actually reflects the island’s car culture.
